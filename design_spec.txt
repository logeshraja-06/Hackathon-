Design Specifications & Wireframes
==================================

Color Palette:
- Primary: #2E7D32 (Dark Green)
- Accent: #E65100 (Deep Orange)
- Background: #FFF8E1 (Light Cream/Yellow)

Typography:
- Title: 22px, Bold, #1B5E20
- Subtitle: 18px, Semi-Bold, #333333
- Body: 16px, Regular, #666666
- Buttons: >=48x48 dp tap target size, 18px bold text.
- Spacing: 16px standard margins/padding across screens.
